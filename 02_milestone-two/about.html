About

This application is designed to provide an overview of the rental sector in Ireland by presenting average rent year on year for the period 2013 to 2017. Using data from the Central Statistics Office, average monthly rent across various property types such as apartments and the number of bedrooms in a property have been aggregated for yearly estimates and are some the variables examined in this application.

For those interested in a more detailed and comprehensive analysis of rent in Ireland, the following resources may be of interest;
  • Tenure and Rent - CSO
  • Housing in Ireland Infographic - CSO
  • Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) Rent Index
