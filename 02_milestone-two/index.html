Property Rental in Ireland 2013 - 2017

A dashboard on average monthly rental for the years 2013 - 2017 according to property type.

This chart shows the total monthly rent according to property type for 2017. It is interesting to note the total for Apartments which is marginally higher than Detached houses and only marginally lower than the total for Semi detached houses.
This chart shows the distribution of the number of rooms per property according to total monthly rent for the year.
This chart displays the average monthly rent total across all years for each property type. We can see Other flat properties remaining consistently the lowest across each of the years, being the only property type to fall in total for 2017 from the previous year.
This chart shows how total monthly rent has overall continued to rise in the previous five years with Apartments and Semi detached properties showing the greatest increases compared to other property types.
